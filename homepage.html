About Jaipur

Welcome to Jaipur, the Pink City of India! Known for its rich cultural heritage, magnificent palaces, and vibrant bazaars, Jaipur is a testament to the royal grandeur of Rajasthan.

Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is renowned for its distinctive pink-colored buildings, traditional handicrafts, and delectable cuisine. As the capital of Rajasthan, it seamlessly blends the allure of its ancient history with modern amenities.

Explore our website to discover the magnificent heritage sites, book your stay in our luxurious hotels, and browse through our gallery showcasing the city's most breathtaking views.
